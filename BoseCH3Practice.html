Our company's mission is to get more kids to play lacrosse and interact with their community

I started my Lacrosse career late in my freshman year of highschool, and it undoubtedly became the focal point of my highschool years. My mission is to help connect young lacrosse players together to help each other train and practice together



Everyone knows a major drawback to the sport of lacrosse is the price of everything you need to start playing. With our website, we don't ask you to purchase a membership or buy any equipment, all you need to start playing is:

  • A lacrosse stick
  • Lacrosse ball
  • Wifi Enabled Device
